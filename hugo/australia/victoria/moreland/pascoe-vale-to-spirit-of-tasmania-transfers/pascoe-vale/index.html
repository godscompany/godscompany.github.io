Tips for Travelling from Pascoe Vale, Victoria to the Spirit of Tasmania Terminal

Planning ahead can make your journey from Pascoe Vale, Victoria to the Spirit of Tasmania ferry terminal in Geelong smooth and stress-free. Whether you’re embarking on a Tasmanian adventure, relocating, or transporting a vehicle, these handy tips will help ensure a seamless experience from your doorstep to the dock:

  • Book Your Transfer Early
    The Spirit of Tasmania terminal can get quite busy, especially during holiday seasons, weekends, or school breaks. Booking your transfer in advance guarantees your preferred pick-up time and allows us to assign the ideal vehicle based on your needs — whether you’re travelling solo, as a couple, or with family and cargo.

  • Know Your Boarding Time
    The ferry has strict boarding cut-off times, particularly for vehicle passengers. Make sure you know your check-in and boarding time well in advance. We recommend allowing extra time for vehicle processing if you’re bringing your car aboard, as queues can form quickly during peak sailings.

  • Leave Ample Buffer Time
    We recommend departing at least 2.5 to 3 hours before your scheduled check-in time, especially if you’re coming from outside Geelong or travelling during peak hours. This buffer ensures you’re covered in case of unexpected traffic or delays, and helps avoid the stress of rushing.

  • Inform Us of Extra Luggage or Special Requirements
    Travelling with bikes, luggage trailers, or pets? Let us know ahead of time so we can send the most appropriate vehicle. We offer spacious vans and pet-friendly options to ensure your ride is both comfortable and well-suited to your travel plans.

  • Travel Off-Peak When Possible
    If you have a flexible schedule, consider booking transfers during off-peak hours. Early mornings and late evenings typically have lighter traffic and quicker processing at the terminal. This can make your trip more relaxed and help you arrive at the ferry feeling fresh and on time.

  • Stay Updated on Traffic and Sailing Conditions
    Geelong traffic and coastal weather can affect travel times. We recommend checking live traffic and ferry updates before your trip. Our team also monitors conditions and can adjust your pickup time if needed to keep your journey on track.
