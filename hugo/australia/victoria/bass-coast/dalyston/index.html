Tips for Travelling from Dalyston, Victoria to Melbourne or Avalon Airport

When it comes to catching a flight, planning ahead can significantly reduce stress — and it’s especially important when you’re travelling from Dalyston, Victoria, which is conveniently located between two major airports, Avalon and Tullamarine. Whether you’re heading out for a quick getaway, a family holiday, or a business trip, these practical tips will help make your airport journey smoother and more enjoyable:

  • Book Early
    Avalon Airport, while smaller and quieter than Tullamarine, can still get busy — particularly during peak times like weekends, school holidays, or special events. By booking your airport transfer in advance, you can secure your preferred time slot and avoid last-minute hassles. This way, you can ensure a more relaxed journey to the airport, knowing your ride is ready and waiting for you.

  • Double-Check Your Terminal
    Knowing your airline’s terminal can save you valuable time and help prevent confusion. Avalon Airport has a more straightforward layout with just one terminal, but Tullamarine is a much larger, busier airport with multiple terminals. Understanding your departure terminal at Tullamarine beforehand will help you navigate the airport with ease, especially during peak traffic hours, so you won’t waste time getting lost or caught up in unnecessary queues.

  • Leave Some Buffer Time
    While Avalon is known for its shorter queues and easier check-ins, it’s always best to arrive early to avoid any potential stress. We recommend leaving at least 2 hours before your domestic flight and at least 3 hours for an international flight. This buffer will give you peace of mind in case of unexpected delays, giving you plenty of time to relax and get to your gate without rushing.

  • Travel Light or Let Us Know About Special Requirements
    If you’re travelling with extra luggage, sporting equipment, or a pram, don’t hesitate to inform us when booking your transfer. Whether you need extra space for golf clubs, skis, or additional bags, we’ll ensure the right vehicle is dispatched to accommodate your needs. By letting us know in advance, we can provide a comfortable and spacious ride that ensures a stress-free experience when loading your luggage.

  • Consider Off-Peak Travel
    If your schedule is flexible, consider travelling during off-peak hours to make your journey to the airport even easier. Early mornings or late evenings tend to have less traffic on the roads, and airport security lines are generally shorter, making for a more relaxed start to your trip. This is especially helpful when travelling to Avalon, where there are limited access routes during peak hours. By avoiding busy times, you’ll be able to breeze through the roads and airport without unnecessary delays.
