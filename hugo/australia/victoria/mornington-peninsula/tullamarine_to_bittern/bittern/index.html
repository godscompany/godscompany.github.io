Tips for Travelling to Bittern from Melbourne or Avalon Airport

Planning your post-flight journey is just as important as planning your trip. Whether you’re flying into Avalon Airport or Melbourne Tullamarine, a smooth ride to Bittern starts with a few simple steps:

  • Book Your Pickup in Advance
    While Avalon is smaller and less crowded than Tullamarine, both airports can get busy — especially during school holidays, weekends, or major events. Booking your transfer in advance ensures a driver is ready to meet you when you land, saving you time and stress. You won’t need to wait for a taxi or navigate rideshare apps after a long flight.

  • Know Your Arrival Terminal
    Avalon Airport has a single terminal, making it easy to locate your driver. Tullamarine, however, has multiple terminals across domestic and international flights. Be sure to check your arrival terminal ahead of time so your driver can meet you in the right location, ensuring a quick and smooth exit from the airport.

  • Allow Time for Baggage and Customs
    While Avalon generally offers a quicker exit process, it’s wise to allow at least 30 minutes post-arrival for baggage claim and, for international flights at Tullamarine, additional time for customs. Let us know your flight number when booking — we track delays and adjust pickup times automatically.

  • Let Us Know About Extra Luggage or Special Needs
    Whether you’re bringing large suitcases, sports gear, or require child seats, informing us ahead of time allows us to assign the right vehicle for your needs. This ensures your ride to Bittern is as comfortable and efficient as possible, with plenty of room and no surprises.

  • Consider Late-Night or Off-Peak Arrivals
    If your flight lands outside of peak traffic hours (early morning or late evening), you’ll likely benefit from a quicker ride to Bittern. Tullamarine and Avalon both experience reduced road congestion during these times, making your transfer more efficient and relaxing.

  • Stay Updated on Local Conditions
    Though we monitor flight times and traffic in real time, it’s helpful to stay informed about local weather and events, especially during holiday periods or major public events in Melbourne. Rest assured, our experienced drivers adapt to road conditions and reroute when necessary to get you home to Bittern as efficiently as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is the SkyBus Avalon City Express, and where does it go?

A: The SkyBus Avalon City Express offers direct transfers between Avalon Airport and Melbourne Southern Cross Station. It’s a reliable public transport option for travelers heading to or from Melbourne CBD. Prices start at around $24 one way. Check their website for the latest timetable and fare info.

Q: Are there public transport options from Avalon Airport to Geelong?

A: Yes. While there is no direct Public Transport Victoria (PTV) train from Avalon Airport, you can connect via bus and train routes. For example, you can catch a bus from Avalon Airport to Lara or Geelong Station and then transfer via V/Line services. Visit PTV’s Journey Planner for accurate connections and schedules.

Q: Where can I find parking options and rates at Avalon Airport?

A: Avalon Airport offers convenient parking just steps from the terminal. You can explore your options and book online at the Avalon Airport Parking page. A detailed list of rates can be found in the official parking schedule PDF. For commonly asked questions, check out the Avalon Parking FAQs.

Q: How do I drive to Avalon Airport?

A: Avalon Airport is easily accessible via the Princes Freeway (M1) between Melbourne and Geelong. Directions and driving tips are available on the Getting Here – Driving page.

Q: Where can I check Avalon Airport flight schedules?

A: You can check the latest flight schedules at Avalon Airport on their official Flight Schedule page.

Q: Where can I find Melbourne Airport departure and arrival information?

A: For up-to-date information on flights departing from Melbourne Airport, visit the Melbourne Airport Departures page. For arrivals, see the Melbourne Airport Arrivals page.

Q: What parking and transport options are available at Melbourne Airport?

A: Melbourne Airport provides a variety of parking options, including value and express parking. For details, visit the Parking & Transport page. Check the Drive-Up Rates for pricing. You can also find information on Taxi services and Bus Services.

Q: How do I get to and from Melbourne Airport?

A: Comprehensive transport options including buses, taxis, and rideshare are listed on the Getting To and From The Airport page.

Q: What are the different parking products available at Melbourne Airport?

A: Melbourne Airport offers several parking products including Value Parking, Value Express, and Terminal Parking.

Q: What facilities and amenities are available at Melbourne Airport?

A: For dining options, visit the Shop & Eat page. Learn more about airport services at the At The Airport page. Find terminal maps on the Terminal Guides page. To relax before your flight, explore Lounges in the Airport. If you need accommodation, check the Accommodation page.

Q: Can I use Uber from Avalon or Melbourne Airport?

A: Yes, Uber operates at both Avalon Airport and Melbourne Airport. Dedicated rideshare pickup zones are available at both airports. You can book a ride through the Uber app and choose from a range of services such as UberX, Comfort, or UberXL depending on your needs and luggage size.

Q: Does DiDi operate from Melbourne or Avalon airports?

A: DiDi is available in Melbourne and may also service nearby areas such as Avalon Airport. Availability can vary based on demand and time of day. Use the DiDi app to check ride options and fare estimates. Note: designated rideshare zones should be used for pickup at major airports.

Q: Can I pre-book a ride with Ola from the airport?

A: Yes, Ola offers pre-booked and on-demand ride services from Melbourne Airport and surrounding areas. While Ola currently has limited presence at Avalon Airport, it’s a popular choice in Melbourne metro and Geelong areas. Download the Ola app to book a ride or check airport pickup zones.

Q: Which ride-share is the cheapest from Melbourne Airport to the city or Geelong?

A: Prices vary depending on time of day, traffic, and surge pricing. It’s recommended to compare rates across the Uber, DiDi, and Ola apps before booking. Sometimes, rideshare services can be more affordable than traditional taxis, especially during off-peak hours.

Q: Are ride-share services available 24/7 at Melbourne and Avalon airports?

A: Yes, all major ride-share platforms like Uber, DiDi, and Ola operate 24/7 at Melbourne Airport. Availability at Avalon Airport may depend on the time and number of drivers in the area, so consider pre-booking or checking in advance during late-night hours.
